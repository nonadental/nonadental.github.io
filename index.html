We are a family-owned and operated dentistry in Farmington Hills, Michigan with two generations in our practice. Dr. Basil E Nona, D.D.S. graduated from University of Detroit Mercy School of Dentistry and has been practicing dental medicine in the Metro-Detroit area for 35+ years. He can communicate with patients in English and Chaldean. Dr. Matthew Nona, D.D.S. is also a graduate of University of Detroit Mercy School of Dentistry and has experience working with patients around the Metro-Detroit area. Together, we take our time to make sure that your smile is healthy, white, fresh, and full and that you leave our office pain-free!
